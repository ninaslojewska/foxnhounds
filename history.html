HISTORY
Fox and Hounds Scottish Pubs was established in Holstebro in 1991 by the
Scottish man Simon Martin. Since has the two pubs in Herning and Aabenraa
been established and lately the fourth in Sønderborg, autumn 2015. Every pub is
operated individually and has its own distinctive character. However, every one is
with the same concept – to sell great quality beers and whisky for the modern
crowd, and thereby promote the beer and whisky culture in Denmark. This
requires a stock that is of the highest quality as well as a diverse and exquisite
range.

We import most of our beers from all over Europe. We also offers a large menu
with delicious courses at reasonable prices in a casual atmosphere.

Originally, Fox and Hounds is a family-owned company, which is symbolized in our
logo, it represents a dog and a fox, that enjoys each others company in front of the
open fire in Holstebro.
