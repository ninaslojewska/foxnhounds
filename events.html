BEER - TASTING

A diverse way of getting together.

Beer-tasting at Fox and Hounds is a diverse and casual way of getting together for
a nice evening with friends, colleagues, family or the association.

While you are tasting exciting national and international beers, one of our talented
bartenders are telling stories of the origin of the beer and the composition, and
about the international breweries, similarities and differences of the beers'
ingredients, brewing methods around the world. You will also be a part of the
impression of taste, scent and colour of the beer.

8 different beer types will be presented and a focus upon the diversity of the beer-
world through diverse types, combinations, brewing methods and processes will
occur. All this is spiced with anecdotes and stories about beers within an inspiring
and humorous way, and of course the mood rises while the beers are enjoyed.

Beer tasting can be combined with food; you can either choose between our famous
tender spareribs or our delicious flammkuchen, which will be served in different
styles until you are full. If you have any specific desires for the beer-tasting, i.e.
specific beers, types or focus areas we will do our best to meet your wishes.

Price: 300,-/person

COCKTAIL - TASTING

An exotic night at Fox and Hounds.

Cocktail-tasting at Fox and Hounds is a diverse way to get together on a cozy
night with friends, colleagues, family or the business. While you taste exciting and
exotic drinks, one of our great bartenders will tell stories upon ingredients,
composition and character.

During the drinks-tasting, a focus is on the explanation of diverse techniques,
which is suitable for different drinks, how to combine the ingredients best to
compose a harmonious drink. Likewise will there be told several background
stories upon origin and development. The bartender will explain which situations
or occasions that are suitable for the different drinks, and how to enjoy them.

Price: 375,-/person
With tapas: 525,-/person

BANQUETING ROOM

Have your next banqueting or meeting with us…
We have the room for it!

Fox and Hounds Sønderborg has a nice and different banqueting room that can
provide the setting for your next banqueting or cozy meeting. With our pleasant
atmosphere and competent staff, we would like to arrange a nice banqueting,
association meeting or the unpretentious business meeting. Maximum occupancy is
40 people.

Our menus are composed based on our modern and classic pub concept, with the
opportunity to enjoy a 3 course menu or buffet with, for example, a Danish or
Scottish feel. Come in for a non-committal talk, where you are also more than
welcome to see our locals.
